Gloo Holdings, Inc.

GLOO | $100.1M

A mid-cap offering with strong institutional backing from Roth Capital Partners. With 9.1M shares priced between $10-12, Gloo Holdings represents a solid growth opportunity. The company is scheduled to trade on Wednesday, November 19th, making it one of the week's key mid-cap plays.

Expected: 11/19/2025 Wednesday | Price Range: $10.00 - $12.00

#4

Public Policy Holding Co.

PPHC | $60.0M

A NASDAQ uplisting with a fixed price offering at $14.64 per share. This public policy consulting firm is making the jump to a major exchange with backing from Oppenheimer & Co., Canaccord Genuity, and Texas Capital Securities. The fixed pricing suggests strong pre-market demand.

Expected: 12/1/2025 Week of | Price Range: $14.64 - $14.64

#5

HW Electro Co., Ltd.

HWEP | $16.6M

A new NASDAQ filing from the electronics sector, this Korean company is entering the U.S. markets with a $16.6M offering. Managed by American Trust Investment Services and WestPark Capital, this represents an international expansion play in the technology hardware space.

Expected: 12/1/2025 Week of | Price Range: $4.00 - $4.00
